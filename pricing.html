SWIFT Payments

Sending money to countries without local rail coverage. Uses the international SWIFT network.

  • OUR (Sender pays all) ~SGD 35.00
  • SHA (Shared costs) ~SGD 10.00
  • BEN (Receiver pays) SGD 0.00
SWIFT fees are charged by correspondent banks. We pass these costs through without markup.

Pricing FAQs

Are there any hidden fees?

Absolutely not. We display the full cost (Exchange Rate + Fee) before you confirm any transaction. What you see is exactly what you pay.

Do you charge for receiving money?

Receiving funds via local rails (e.g. FAST in Singapore, ACH in USA) is free. Receiving funds via SWIFT may incur a small inward processing fee charged by our banking partner, typically around $5-10.

Can I negotiate better rates?

Yes. If your business transacts large volumes (over $100k/month), please contact our sales team. We can offer custom pricing tiers tailored to your flow.
