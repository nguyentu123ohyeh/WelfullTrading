Fabric Export

As a leading fabric exporter in Vietnam, we offer a comprehensive portfolio of fabrics for diverse applications including garments, industrial uses, and home textiles. Our extensive sourcing network connects you with reputable manufacturers and mills across Asia, ensuring a wide selection of materials such as cotton, polyester, spandex, denim, canvas, and specialty blends.

We pride ourselves on our ability to provide fully customized solutions, tailoring fabric color, pattern, weight, and technical specifications to match your unique requirements. Every shipment is subject to rigorous quality control procedures and international certifications (OEKO-TEX, ISO, SGS), guaranteeing consistent quality and compliance.

Our experienced export team manages all aspects of the process, from order confirmation, production monitoring, and pre-shipment inspection to international logistics and customs clearance. We maintain transparent communication throughout, providing timely updates and documentation to ensure peace of mind for our clients.

  • Extensive range of fabrics: apparel, industrial, and home textiles
  • Full customization on color, design, and technical specs
  • Strict quality assurance and international certifications
  • Efficient export logistics and global distribution

Partner with us for a seamless, reliable, and cost-effective fabric sourcing and export experience that empowers your business to grow globally.

Trade Finance

Our trade finance solutions help businesses navigate the complexities of international payments, credit, and risk management. We offer expert consulting on a full range of payment methods, including Letters of Credit (L/C), Telegraphic Transfer (T/T), Documents against Payment (D/P), and Documents against Acceptance (D/A), tailored to your specific transaction needs.

We work closely with major domestic and international banks to facilitate smooth, secure cross-border payments and provide banking guarantees where required. Our team assists in preparing documentation, conducting due diligence, and ensuring full compliance with international trade regulations.

In addition, we provide risk assessment and mitigation strategies to protect your business from fraud, non-payment, and currency fluctuations, giving you the confidence to expand your trade operations globally.

  • Comprehensive international payment solutions (L/C, T/T, D/P, D/A)
  • Financial risk assessment and compliance advisory
  • Banking support and guarantee issuance
  • Fraud prevention and transaction security

Secure and streamline your global business transactions with our professional trade finance expertise.
