FX Tools for Business

Spot FX

Immediate

Buy or sell currency for immediate delivery. The rate is fixed now, and settlement happens within 2 business days (usually T+0 or T+1).

Best used for:

  • Urgent supplier invoices
  • Immediate payroll needs
  • Taking advantage of a sudden rate drop

FX Forwards

Hedging

Lock in an exchange rate today for a settlement date in the future (up to 12 months). Protect your business from adverse market movements.

Best used for:

  • Budgeting for future inventory
  • Protecting profit margins on long contracts
  • Managing cash flow without tying up capital (Small deposit required)
