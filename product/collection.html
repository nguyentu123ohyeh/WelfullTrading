Collection FAQs

Are these real bank accounts?

They are "Virtual Accounts" mapped to Wallex's master accounts at top-tier banks. Functionally, they work exactly like real bank accounts for receiving funds via local payment networks.

Can I hold the money I receive?

Yes. Unlike many competitors who force-convert your funds immediately, Wallex allows you to hold USD, EUR, GBP, etc., indefinitely in your multi-currency wallet.
