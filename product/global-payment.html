Payment Capabilities

Engineered for businesses with high-volume needs.

1. Local Rails (Domestic)

We bypass the SWIFT network by having local bank accounts in 47+ countries. When you pay a supplier in the UK, we pay them from our London account via FPS/CHAPS.

  • • Zero to low fees
  • • Full value received (No deduction)

2. SWIFT gpi (International)

For countries where local rails aren't available, we use SWIFT gpi. It's the gold standard for tracking international wires.

  • • End-to-end tracking ID
